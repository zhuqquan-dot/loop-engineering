# Judgment Journal

> Once per week. This is your last piece of evidence that you are still the engineer.
> Three questions. If you cannot write anything — you are in cognitive surrender.

## Template

```markdown
## W{week} ({date})

### Q1: What did I reject this week? Why?
[At least 1 item. If everything passed, you were not paying attention or the evaluator is too lenient]

### Q2: What did I approve this week but was unsure about?
[Be honest. This question is the most valuable one]

### Q3: If this loop disappeared tomorrow, could I keep working?
[No — you have been replaced by it]
```
